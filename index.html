What Victoria's House Prices Look Like

Notice in the map below that the darker shades of red indicate that there is a further distance to travel from the suburb to the city. Also the size of the dots depicts how expensive a suburb is

Notice that the most expensive suburb in Victoria is Kooyong which represents the Stonnington City Council. What is important is how the most expensive suburbs mostly come from the Boroondara City Council

Notice that the most affordable suburbs in Victoria are Brookfield, Coolaroo and Wyndham Vale. What is important is how the most affordable suburbs mostly come from the Melton City Council. Also it seems that Melbourne's most affordable suburbs are from the west side of the CBD
